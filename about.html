I am a man of many hustles who approaches all projects and challenges with the flexible, creative mindset of an artist and the analytical, researched-based focus of an engineer.

I received a Bachelor's degree in Art Practice at Stanford University, with a specialization in creative writing, graphic narrative, and painting. I've since taken my experience to education and front-end development, and am currently transitioning into the tech sphere.

My combination of professional experience and my personal experience as a queer, trans, Filipino man creates a unique perspective that informs my work in all fields. My work is primarily informed by interests rather than an adherence to a particular form or context. I have explored science fiction, Filipino American history, work in nonprofit fields, holistic learning, mental health, radical self-determination, and human-centered design.
