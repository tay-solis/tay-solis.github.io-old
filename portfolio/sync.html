Sync

Sync is a 4 page comic created for Stack Deck Press's We're Still Here anthology.

Set in a science fiction future featuring robot boxing matches, Sync follows Tanza de la Cruz, an athlete whose gender identity has brought him to the center of attention. He balances the scrutiny and transphobia of others with his love of the sport and the love of becoming synchronized with his mech, which he sees as a more true extension of his body.
